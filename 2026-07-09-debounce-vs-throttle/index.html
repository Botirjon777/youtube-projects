Fires on every event. Hundreds of times. This is what kills performance.

THROTTLEat most once per interval0

A steady heartbeat — runs regularly while you move. Great for scroll & resize.

DEBOUNCEonce, after you stop0

Waits for quiet, then fires once. Perfect for search-as-you-type & autosave.

When to use which

  • Debounce → search boxes, autosave, validating a field, resize-then-recalculate. "Do it once the user is done."
  • Throttle → scroll position, mousemove tracking, rate-limiting clicks, infinite scroll. "Do it regularly, but not too often."
