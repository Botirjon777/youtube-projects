# 🎞️ Sci-Fi Movie Backgrounds

> Pillar: **Creative / Fan** · Publish: **2026-07-18**

Iconic sci-fi films as mouse-reactive hero backgrounds — **three** in one page, pure `<canvas>`, zero libraries.

## ▶️ Run it
Open `index.html`. Move your mouse. Switch films with the pills.

## 🎬 The films
| Film | Reacts to mouse by… |
|------|---------------------|
| 🕳️ **Interstellar** | **Gargantua** — a black hole whose **accretion disk + lensing halo** wrap around your cursor, over a starfield |
| 🌀 **Inception** | the **infinite loop** — rotating squares zoom outward forever from your cursor, with a spinning **totem** |
| 🌃 **Blade Runner** | **neon rain city** — flickering neon haze + skyline; your cursor is the **searchlight**, rain blows with your mouse |

## 🎬 Video outline (Short)
1. Hook (0–2s): the black hole forming around the cursor — instantly recognizable.
2. (2–10s): Interstellar → Inception → Blade Runner, one beat each.
3. (8–14s): on Inception, hold still and let the infinite zoom hypnotize.
4. CTA: "Name the movie 🎬 — which world is your wallpaper?"

## 🗣️ Talking points
- Interstellar's look = a squashed **accretion-disk ellipse** + a **non-squashed bright halo arc** over the top (the gravitational-lensing trick), then a solid black event horizon on top.
- Inception = squares whose size grows with `s²` so they **accelerate outward**, recycling at the center → a seamless endless loop.
- Blade Runner layers it: fade → additive neon haze → skyline silhouette → cursor searchlight → foreground rain.
